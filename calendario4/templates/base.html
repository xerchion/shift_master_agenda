Shift Management:

Easily generate a dynamic calendar/agenda for varying work shifts:

Morning, afternoon and night

Go to Agenda

Color-Coded Shifts:

Each work shift is associated with a customizable color, making it easy to differentiate between different shifts.

Change colours

Holiday Marking:

The app calculates holidays and highlights them on the calendar for quick reference.

It also takes into account the holidays that the user works.

Change colours

Altered days Marking:

The days that the user modifies are highlighted with underlining.

And, of course, rest days are also reflected.

Change colours

User Profiles:

The app includes tables in the database to store user profiles, making it easy to manage and assign shifts. Tailor the app to your preferences through the user configuration screen. Additionally, the user can adjust shifts, apply bonuses, and manage overtime hours, among other features.

Monthly and yearly summaries:

The app also includes total or partial summaries of the total days per shift, breaks, bonuses, and overtime hours
